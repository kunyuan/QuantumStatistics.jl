QuantumStatistics.Spectral.kernelFermiT — Method
kernelFermiT(τ, ω)

Compute the imaginary-time fermionic kernel. Assume $k_B T/\hbar=1$

\[g(τ>0) = e^{-ωτ}/(1+e^{-ω}), g(τ≤0) = -e^{-ωτ}/(1+e^{ω})\]

Arguments

  • τ: the imaginary time, must be (-1, 1]
  • ω: frequency
